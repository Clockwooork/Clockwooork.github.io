"It's not our business to make armed superpowers behave; let them fight. I don't care how many generals are going to be hunted down by drones in Moscow, Beijing or Washington. We're on the side of the people. Never forget that."

- Marwen Cannizzaro, Captain of the Meteorina Rescue Ship

In the year 2090, the European Union is more fragile than ever. National governments are struggling to keep up with climate emergencies, every authority is disputed and several regions and cities are claiming independence. The Mediterranean Sea has become a patchwork of unstable environments and even precarious agreements among polities.

It's in these seas the the Meteorina's corsairs sail: they're on a mission to put plasters on disasters, whether by extreme events, extreme ideologies or extreme incompetence.

Meet the Crew!


⚧️ Cpt Canni [Marwen Cannizzaro]

From: Lipari (Aeolian Islands)

Background: Former naval engineer in Sicily

Ethnicity: Tunisian-Sicilian

Personality: Flamboyant and humorous, but also decisive and resourceful. Under pressure they tend to become bossy and sassy.

Role on board: Captain (duh)


♂️ Pietro Furlan

From: Free City of Trieste

Background: Accademia Navale Internazionale di Trieste. Former third electrical engineer on the megafreighter ship Silna.

Ethnicity: Friulian

Personality: Calm and calculating, he tends to follow rules. Extremely dedicated to safety and accuracy in regulations.

Role on board: Mathematician and second engineer


♀️ Keibi [Khadidja Bouzidi]

From: Annaba (Algeria)

Background: Field nurse

Ethnicity: Amazigh

Personality: Chatty, frank and irreverent, level-headed but always ready to crack a joke.

Role on board: Ship's doctor

From: Durrës (Albania)

Background: Rescue diver

Ethnicity: Amazigh

Personality: Simple-minded, straightforward and immune to tension. Loves beers and hates being rushed.

In the current decade, publishing a book or story is increasingly hard, and after a year of bad experiences I have given up on publishers. Instead, I chose to collect all my books and stories on this site, where everything is freely downloadable in every format you need. These stories are for everyone.

If you found this link on a QR code somewhere around the city, I invite you to read something and send me a mail with your opinions! I want to know who's curious enough to dive into the worlds I'm creating.

I can't help it I love surveys!



In the current decade, publishing a book or story is increasingly hard. I refuse to become a peasant on Amazon's self-publishing platform, which is designed to keep authors shackled there under the fake promise of relevance and earnings. Instead, I chose to collect all my books and stories on this site, a platform of my own where everything is freely downloadable in every format you need. These stories are for everyone.

If you found this link on a QR code somewhere around the city, I invite you to read something and send me a mail with your opinions! I want to know who's curious enough to dive into the worlds I'm creating.

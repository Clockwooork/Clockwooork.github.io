In the current decade, publishing a book or story is increasingly hard; after a year of bad experiences and frustrations I have given up on publishers. I also refuse to become a vassal of Amazon's self-publishing platform. Instead, I chose to collect all my books and stories on this site, a platform of my own where everything is freely downloadable in every format you need. These stories are for everyone.

If you found this link on a QR code somewhere around the city, I invite you to read something and send me a mail with your opinions! I want to know who's curious enough to dive into the worlds I'm creating.
